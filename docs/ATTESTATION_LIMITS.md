Receipt Attestation Limits
==========================

Status: skeleton only.

A receipt conformance pack can verify receipt form, schema compatibility, declared fields, fixture expectations, and public validation behavior.

It does not prove:

- truth
- correctness
- legal compliance
- security
- performance
- admission of source material
- absence of private-corpus leakage
- real-world external adoption
- that a product/runtime behaved as claimed outside the supplied receipt and fixture boundary

This document is a placeholder for the full attestation-limit policy.
